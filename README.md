## The Story

You fall asleep at your desk — homework unfinished, test tomorrow, phone buzzing with unread messages. When you open your eyes, you're standing at the entrance to a dungeon.

This is the **Mindscape**: your own mind made visible. Every fear you've been carrying — the exam dread, the crowded hallway, the stage, the spotlight — has taken shape as a chamber with a guardian inside.

A small glowing figure appears. The **Spark** — a fragment of your own self-belief.

*"You've been running from these rooms for a long time. Tonight, we walk through them."*

## How to Play

Open `index.html` in Chrome or Safari. No installation, no internet required.

**Keyboard**
- `Arrow keys` — navigate menus
- `Enter` or `Z` — confirm
- `Space` — advance dialogue
- `M` — mute / unmute

**Mouse** — click anything on screen

**In battle**, choose from your available abilities each turn. Manage your **Courage Points (CP)** — if they hit zero, it's game over. Abilities cost **Calm** (your energy bar), so pick your moments.

Your **★ Sin Miedo** (Fearless) Limit Break charges automatically every 4 turns — free to use, clears all debuffs, and hits hard. Save it for when things get dark.

## Abilities

Abilities unlock as you progress. They're all real techniques for managing anxiety, wrapped in JRPG mechanics.

| Ability | What it does |
|---------|-------------|
| Breathe Deep | Restore Calm. Classic. Always works. |
| Speak Up | Basic courage strike. Say the thing. |
| Reframe | Attack + reduce enemy Dread. Change the narrative. |
| Ground Yourself | 5-4-3-2-1. Restore Calm and CP. |
| Self-Talk | Challenge the thought. Medium damage + weaken boss. |
| Find Your People | Call an ally for a bonus attack. You're not alone. |
| Self-Compassion | Restore CP + gentle attack. Be kind to yourself. |
| Power Pose | Attack + Inspired buff. Your body believes it first. |
| Believe In Yourself | Big attack + restore CP. |
| Break Free | Ultimate strike. Desperation bonus when HP is low. |
| ★ Sin Miedo | **Limit Break.** Charges every 4 turns. Massive damage, clears all debuffs. Free. |
